Your film deserves an audience.

We're here to give you one.

Arthouse exists to champion bold, personal filmmaking from the next generation. We believe in experimentation, imperfection, and the soul behind the shot. We’re not here for trends — we’re here for truth on screen. Submit work that says something, feels something, risks something.

Every film submitted is watched by one of our curators — working filmmakers, editors, or critics — who provide thoughtful, constructive feedback. Selected works appear in The Drop, our weekly curation of emerging shorts.

Submission Guidelines

  • Eligibility: Open to filmmakers aged 16–25. Submissions must be your original work. Collaborative pieces are welcome as long as all contributors are credited.
  • Length: Short films up to 15 minutes (including credits).
  • Genre & Style: All genres, all tones, all mediums. Narrative, documentary, experimental, animation — we want work with perspective, voice, and emotion.
  • Submission Format: Upload your film as a private Vimeo or YouTube link (password-protected if you prefer). Include a short description (100 words max) and a still or poster image.
  • Language: Non-English films must include English subtitles.
  • Rights: You retain full ownership of your work. By submitting, you grant Arthouse non-exclusive rights to stream your film on our platform and feature excerpts for promotional purposes.
  • Originality: Do not submit content you don’t own or control. Any copyrighted material (music, clips, imagery) must be cleared by you.
  • Feedback: Every submission receives a curator review within 10 days. Reviews are private by default unless your film is selected for The Drop.
  • Selection: Films chosen for The Drop will be featured on the site for one week, alongside a written critique and community rating. Selected filmmakers will be contacted directly.
  • Resubmission: You may submit the same project for future Drops if significant changes have been made.

Our Ethos

We don’t believe in gatekeeping, but we do believe in taste. What we value most is voice — your unique way of seeing the world. We want work that makes people feel something they didn’t expect to. That could mean a single frame that lingers, a story told with nothing but silence, or a chaotic burst of color that refuses to resolve. Bring the things that keep you up at night. Bring the work that could only come from you.

Legal & Rights Notice

By submitting, you confirm that:

  • You hold all necessary rights to the work submitted.
  • You grant Arthouse a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to stream, host, and promote your film within the platform and associated channels.
  • Arthouse may use excerpts, stills, or quotes from your film or its review for editorial or promotional purposes.
  • You understand that submission does not guarantee publication or selection in The Drop.
  • All personal data collected will be used solely for the purpose of evaluating and communicating about your submission.

We respect artistic integrity. If your work is selected, you will always be credited as the filmmaker. You may request removal of your film from the platform at any time.
