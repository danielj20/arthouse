Submissions.

general guidelines

At arthouse, we're looking for the next generation of artists, and the next generation of art. Whatever you think that means is exactly what it does. We're not looking for recycled renditions of stories you heard in English class; we're not looking for traces of a painting you had on a Pinterest board. Show us something that's never been seen before, and you'll have good odds of making the curated release at the end of the week.

categories

We accept submissions in the following categories: music, art, fiction, cinema, and poetry. Each category has its own submission guidelines, so be sure to read them carefully before submitting:

  • Music: 6 minutes in duration or less (.mp3 or .wav accepted)
  • Fiction: 4000 words or less (.txt accepted)
  • Poetry: 300 words or less (.txt accepted)
  • Art: high-resolution image (.png, .jpeg accepted)
  • Cinema: 6 minutes in duration or less (.mov or .mp4 accepted)

additional notes

All submissions must be the original work of the submitting artist. By submitting your work, you affirm that you hold all necessary rights to the content and that it does not infringe upon the rights of any third party.


We accept submissions from artists worldwide. However, please note that all submissions must be in English or include an English translation.


By submitting your work, you agree to our terms and conditions, which can be found on our website. Please read them carefully before submitting.
